StreakFlow

Goal tracking with built in motivation.

Everyone knows that practice makes perfect. The problem is keeping the motivation, especially when it's difficult to see the positive effects instantly. This makes achieving your goals hard. Streakflow makes it easy. By tracking your streak of completions, you'll find yourself not wanting that streak to go to zero. Simply, once you start streaking, you won't want to stop. Register now.

How it Works

Create Your Goals

Let's say you want to work on your Spanish, get better at running, and connect with friends better. Your goals would be:
Practice Spanish
Run 1 Mile
Get Dinner with old Friends

Decide How Often

These goals don't require the same amount of effort to be successful. Some should be completed more often than others. After thinking about what you want to achieve, you come up with the following.
Practice Spanish: 1 time per day
Run 1 Mile: 3 times per week
Get Dinner with old Friends: 2 times per month

Mark off Completions

When you complete the goal, click the box to mark it off. You get credit for each completion. But if you don't finish what you set out to complete in the time frame you chose, you go back down to zero. Don't let this happen.

Why it's Effective

Simplicity

The interface is dead simple. Just click a box to mark the objective complete. No complicated interfaces. No confusing settings. And best of all, No gimmicks.

Numeric Feedback

By having a constant reminder of how far you've come, it makes it tougher for you to justify missing the current timeframe.

Consistent Successes

Being able to click a success box gives you a sense that you're achieving your goals, even when other types of feedback are tougher to see visually. And you actually are working towards your goals! Seeing that number increase on a regular basis makes you want to keep working.

You're in Control

There are no outside pressures with Streakflow. You control everything from what the goal is, to how much work you put into it. This means no outside pressure and no one looking over you. Intrinsic work the whole way.
