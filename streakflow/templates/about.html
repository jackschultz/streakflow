StreakFlow

Everyone knows that practice makes perfect. The problem is that keeping the motivation, especially when it's difficult to see the positive effects instantly, makes achieving your goals hard. Streakflow makes it easy. By creating manageable goals, and tracking consecutive completions, you're able to see how easy it is to succeed every day. And once you start streaking, you won't want to stop.

Variable Length Timeframes

Some tasks take longer to do, and some shouldn't be done everyday. Even so, they're still important to get done on a regular basis. Use daily, weekly and monthly time frames when necessary.

Multiple Completions per Timeframe

Sometimes, you may want to complete a goal more than once in a timeframe. With StreakFlow, you're able to decide how many times during your selected timeframe you need for it to be considered successful. As long as you know your goals are beforehand, you'll still keep the streak.

How it works

Step 1: Create a goal

Simply specify the desired time frame, and the number of times in that frame that you want to complete to be successful.

Step 2: Mark your completions

Once you've completed your goal in the timeframe, click on the box to show it's completed.

Step 3: Don't stop; keep streaking

Watch as your consecutive days' completed streak grows bigger. Your only goal now is to not have it go back to 0. Skipping one frame makes it easier to skip the next. Keep working.
By keeping the streak, succeeding comes naturally. Register now.

Why it's Effective

Numeric Feedback

By having a constant reminder of how far you've come, it makes it tougher for you to justify missing the current timeframe.

Consistant Successes

Being able to click a success box gives you a sense that you're achieving your goals, even when other types of feedback are tougher to see visually. And seeing that number increse on a regular basis makes you want to keep working.
