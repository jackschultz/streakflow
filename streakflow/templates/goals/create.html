Tips on goalmaking:

The name should be general. Since you choose frequency and count, there's no need to put numeric constraints in the name.
Start goals off with simple objectives. There's no need to go crazy at the beginning. Sure you don't get credit for any more work over than what you chose, but the goal is consistancy. If you can't meet your expectations, you still go to zero. If you find that you're easily able to complete the goals in your time frame, you can always edit your goal to make it harder. But you can never make it easier!
Some examples: work out, read books, practice guitar, practice foreign language, meditate, volunteer
